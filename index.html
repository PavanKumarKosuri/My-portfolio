Employee Registration Portal

The Employee Registration Portal empowers employees to manage their health checkup experience. They can select their preferred testing method (on-site, off-site, or home collection), add or remove dependents, manage upcoming appointments, review past bookings, and receive a confirmation with a unique booking ID. Razorpay integration facilitates secure payment processing for dependent services.

Phlebotomist Dashboard

Designed to streamline the sample collection process, the Phlebotomist Dashboard provides a personalized workspace for each phlebotomist. After logging in, they can access their assigned employee list, scheduled slots, and appointment history. Initiating a test involves simply entering the employee's booking ID.
